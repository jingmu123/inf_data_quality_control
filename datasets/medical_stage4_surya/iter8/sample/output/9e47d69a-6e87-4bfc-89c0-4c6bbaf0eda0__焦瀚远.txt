【1】##Synkinesis: Basics You Need To Know

【2】1. Symptoms of synkinesis only show on the side of the face affected by Bell's palsy.
2. Facial palsy patients notice symptoms largely in the cheek, chin, and eye and neck area.

【3】3. Even the mild symptoms appear more prominent when the good side is compared to the affected side of the face.

【4】##The Look And Feel

【5】The condition may show up in different ways. The most common observations include:

【6】1. The eye squints whenever you smile.
2. Your cheek might rise as you try to close your eyes.

【7】3. Your neck feels tight when you try to whistle.

【8】4. Facial twitching, especially in the neck and chin area becomes more common.
5. Facial muscles typically in the forehead and eyebrow area become tight, causing discomfort. You may find it difficult to move tight and stiff muscles.
6. Even when the affected side of the face is relaxed, the mouth appears to be pulled up. Your cheek may appear bulky, and the eyes might squint.
