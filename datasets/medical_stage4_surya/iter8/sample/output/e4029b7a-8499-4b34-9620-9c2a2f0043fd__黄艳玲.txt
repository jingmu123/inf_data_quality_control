Pregnant women, especially during the third trimester of pregnancy or moms who have recently given birth are at greater risk of developing Bell’s palsy. The condition however does not appear to have any effect on the developing fetus.

【1】##2. Diabetics

【2】Diabetes is seen as a potential trigger for Bell's palsy. Researchers believe that the condition is related to the damage of blood vessels caused by diabetes.

【3】3. Patients with upper respiratory tract infections Patients with URIs, Upper Respiratory Tract Infections such as cold or flu are also at greater risk of developing Bell’s palsy. The exact reason is unknown, but researchers have linked it to a weakened immune system that allows dormant viruses to attack the 7th cranial nerve.黄艳玲
