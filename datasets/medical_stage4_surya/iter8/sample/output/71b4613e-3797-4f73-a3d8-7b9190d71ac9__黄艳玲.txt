【5】##55. What Is “Respiratory Failure”?

【6】Respiratory failure occurs when the lungs and respiratory system become unable to provide the body with sufficient oxygen and fail to "blow off" accumulated carbon dioxide. If the major disturbance is primarily an inability of the respiratory system to meet the body's oxygen requirement, then "hypoxemic" respiratory failure is present.
