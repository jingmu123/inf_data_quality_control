【19】Administration: Adult

【20】Solution is not for continuous perfusion; leave solution in the organ vasculature during hypothermic storage and transportation. Perfuse with a maximum hydrostatic pressure of 120 mm Hg. **Not** for IV or intra-arterial administration.

【21】Administration: Pediatric

【22】Solution is not for continuous perfusion; leave solution in the organ vasculature during hypothermic storage and transportation. Perfuse with a maximum hydrostatic pressure of 120 mm Hg. Not for IV or intra-arterial administration.

【23】Use: Labeled Indications

【24】**Donor organ perfusion:** Flushing and perfusion of donor heart, kidneys, liver, and pancreas prior to or immediately after removal from the donor.

【25】Medication Safety Issues

【26】High alert medication:

【27】The Institute for Safe Medication Practices (ISMP) includes this medication among its list of drug classes (cardioplegic solution) which have a heightened risk of causing significant patient harm when used in error (High-Alert Medications in Acute Care Settings).

【28】International issues:

【29】Custodiol \[US, Canada, and multiple international markets\] is also the brand name for bisacodyl \[Indonesia\].
