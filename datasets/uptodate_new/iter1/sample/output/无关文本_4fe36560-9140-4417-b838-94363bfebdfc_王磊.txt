【7】Index Terms

【8】*   Tropisetron Hydrochloride

【9】Pharmacologic Category

【10】*   Antiemetic; Selective 5-HT3 Receptor Antagonist

【11】Reported Use

【12】Prevention of nausea and vomiting induced by cytotoxic chemotherapy; treatment and prevention of postoperative nausea/vomiting

【13】Dosage Range

【14】Adults:

【15】IV:

【16】Chemotherapy-induced nausea and vomiting: 5 mg before chemotherapy on day 1.

【17】Prevention of postoperative nausea/vomiting: 2 mg shortly before induction of anesthesia.

【18】Treatment of postoperative nausea/vomiting: 2 mg as a single dose.

【19】Oral: Chemotherapy-induced nausea and vomiting: 5 mg daily on days 2 through 6 following IV dose given on day 1.

【20】Product Availability

【21】Product available in various countries; not currently available in the US

【22】Dosage Forms

【23】Solution, Injection: 1 mg/mL

【24】Solution, Injection/Oral: 1 mg/mL
